NASA's Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope is closer to launch with the completion of its spacecraft bus. This "RV" of space exploration will transport the telescope, provide power, communication, and data transmission, and enable its scientific mission to study dark energy, dark matter, and exoplanets. The spacecraft will collect and transmit 1.4 terabytes of data daily, surpassing other space telescopes. With nearly 50 miles of electrical cabling and deployable elements like solar panels and antennas, the spacecraft bus is a complex system that will support the telescope's ambitious goals.
